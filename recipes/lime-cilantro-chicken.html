Slow Cooker Cilantro Lime Chicken

Description

This slow cooker cilantro-lime chicken is bursting with flavor! Try it in tacos for a nice change from plain chicken.

This easy-to-make recipe will be your new go-to chicken recipe. Say goodbye to bland chicken with this recipe that can be easily made. With about fifteen minutes of prep time, this recipes supports even the busiest of lifestyles.

Serves approximately 12 people.

Ingredients

The following ingredients will be required for your flavorful chicken:

  • One 16 oz jar of salsa
  • One 1.25 oz package of dry taco seasoning mix
  • One medium lime, juiced
  • Three tablespoons of chopped fresh cilantro
  • Three pounds skinless, boneless chicken thighs

Steps

Now that you have all the ingredients, here is what you need to do to prepare your new favorite dish:

  1. Gather all ingredients
  2. Stir salsa, taco seasoning, lime juice, and cilantro together in a slow cooker until well combined. Add chicken and spoon salsa mixture over top to coat.
  3. Cover and cook until chicken is no longer pink in the center and the juices run clear, on Low for 6 to 8 hours or High for 4 hours. An instant-read thermometer inserted into the center should read at least 165 degrees F (74 degrees C).
  4. Shred chicken in the crock with two forks, then mix with the sauce
  5. Enjoy your meal!
